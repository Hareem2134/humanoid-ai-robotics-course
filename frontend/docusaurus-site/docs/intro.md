Welcome to "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics: A Comprehensive Guide". This book is designed to provide industry practitioners with a comprehensive understanding of humanoid robotics and physical AI.

Throughout this book, we will cover a wide range of topics, from the fundamentals of ROS 2 to the complexities of Vision-Language-Action models. Our goal is to provide you with the knowledge and skills necessary to design, build, and program humanoid robots.
